How to Use This Panel

Quick Instructions:

  • Toggle Days: Use the switch to enable/disable availability for each day
  • Add Times: Click "Add Time Slot" to add available appointment times
  • Remove Times: Click the red "×" button to remove unwanted time slots
  • Save Changes: Click "Save All Changes" to update your website
  • Time Format: Use 24-hour format (e.g., 19:00 for 7:00 PM)
  • Advance Booking: Customers must book at least 1 day in advance
